Admission


ADMISSION:

  • STEP 1

    • Parents / Guardian visit or call the school to set the assessment appointment.


      • Assessment apointment is to make sure that the student is qualified to ente the grade that he/she is suppose to enter.


  • STEP 2

    • - Once appointment is set, the child will be sit for assessment test.


      • Paper test concist of ENGLISH, MATH & SCIENCE.

      • Placement Interview (Reading Skills & Moral Knowledge)


        • NOTE: Upon asssessment, parents will be required to pay for the Assement fee ($USD 70)

  • STEP 3

    • Once assessment evaluated by the teacher, the parents will be inform the result via telephone call or email.


      • Successful students placement will be based on the available seats.

Registration

1.

(a) No student shall be registered unless the parent or guardian is either a member or associate member of the School and Parents Association (SPA) or gives signed evidence of compliance with the School Articles of the Constitution.

2.

(b) Acceptance of students shall be by the Board or by persons appointed by the Board for that purpose.

3.

(c)The Board has the right to suspend or dismiss any student from the school form cause. Reasons for suspension or dismissal shall be communicated to the parents in writing.

4.

(d)Children are admitted to the School into the class appropriate to their age. Parents are required to produce evidence of age when submitting the application form. The academic year is from the beginning of September of one year to the end of August of the next and this constitutes the cutoff point when making decisions as to the appropriate year group.
